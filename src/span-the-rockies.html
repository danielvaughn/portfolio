SPAN The Rockies 2012

SPAN (Safehouse Progressive Alliance for Non-violence) is a non-profit center in Boulder that provides a safe haven for victims of domestic assault. In 2012 they hired me to redesign their annual cycling tour. While I completed their logo and peripherals, they decided not to complete the redesign of the tour's website.
