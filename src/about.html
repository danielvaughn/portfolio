Background

I'm an artist and always have been. I graduated college in 2008 with a Fine Arts degree. In 2011, I realized that I needed a website to showcase my paintings. Being resourceful (and, to be honest, a bit picky) I decided to try building it myself. I had never touched a line of code until then, but once I did, I was mesmerized. The creative power afforded to me by software was something I had never experienced before. I was hooked.


After that first website, I became so obsessed that I switched careers and taught myself how to program. That was in 2011, and since that first line of code, I've been busy. Here's what I've been up to these last few years.
